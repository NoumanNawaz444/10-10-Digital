Do we use Cookies?

Yes we do. We use a cookie to track how customers and prospective customers engage and use our website. This is so we can improve our website and customer experience.

Session cookies typically are stored on your computer and then deleted once the browser changes or closes and they do not collect personal information to profile you.

Persistent cookies are stored on a file inside your computer even after your close your web browser, which differs from session cookies.
