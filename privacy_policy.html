  • Aggregated Data – this is a form of data that does not identify you directly but is data combined from usage of our services such as our website so we can identify if something is working or not. If we ever translate aggregated data to identify any user of our services then it is and will be treated as personal data. Contact Data: this includes data such as e-mail addresses and methods to contact you such as phone number and address.
  • Marketing – how you have chosen to interact with us such as subscriptions to our newsletters, communications from us such as marketing information or communications from our trusted partners. Purpose – why you are interacting with us, such as a job application, including information stored in your CV, using our online education services, signing up to attend an event, or how you use our websites & services.
